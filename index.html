Frequently Asked Questions

Is this software free?

Home Inventory Manager is free to use. You can help support me at PayPal or Venmo.

Where is my data stored?

Your inventory database and photos are stored locally on your computer in ~/.local/share/home-inventory-app/ (Linux) or your user directory (Windows). No data is sent to external servers.

Can I backup my data?

Yes! Simply export your inventory to CSV or PDF, or copy the entire application data folder to backup your database and photos.
